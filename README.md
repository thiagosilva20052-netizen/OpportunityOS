# OpportunityOS

> Researching opportunities before building solutions.

## Overview

OpportunityOS is a research project focused on developing a systematic methodology for discovering, evaluating, and prioritizing business opportunities before building products, services, or startups.

Instead of starting with ideas, technologies, or assumptions, this project starts with evidence: real market problems, unmet needs, recurring frictions, and observable patterns.

The objective is to reduce entrepreneurial risk by helping founders identify opportunities that are worth solving.

---

## Why this project exists

Many startups fail because they begin with a solution and then search for a problem.

OpportunityOS proposes the opposite approach:

1. Discover real market problems.
2. Understand recurring patterns.
3. Evaluate opportunities objectively.
4. Select the best opportunity.
5. Only then design the solution.

The methodology is intended to be independent of any specific technology, industry, or business model.

---

## Core Principles

- Problems before ideas.
- Evidence before assumptions.
- Methodology before technology.
- Patterns before opinions.
- Validation before implementation.

---

## Current Status

This repository is under active research.

The methodology is being developed incrementally through documented phases.

The current focus is building a framework for identifying and evaluating business opportunities based on real market evidence.

## Research Philosophy

This repository does not collect ideas.

It collects knowledge that has survived observation, criticism, experimentation, and validation.

---

## Long-Term Vision

The long-term goal is to create an open, practical, and evidence-based methodology that helps entrepreneurs, investors, consultants, and organizations discover better business opportunities before investing significant time or capital.
