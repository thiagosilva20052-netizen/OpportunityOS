# 1. Project Vision

OpportunityOS is a long-term research project dedicated to developing a systematic, evidence-based methodology for discovering, evaluating, and prioritizing business opportunities before creating products, services, or companies.

The purpose of the project is not to generate ideas.

The purpose is to reduce uncertainty when deciding which opportunity deserves to become a business.

---

# 2. Mission

Create a repeatable methodology that enables entrepreneurs, investors, consultants, and organizations to identify high-potential business opportunities based on real market evidence instead of intuition.

---

# 3. Problem Statement

Most entrepreneurs start with an idea.

Later they try to find customers.

OpportunityOS reverses this process.

Instead of asking:

"What business should I build?"

The methodology asks:

"What important problem is already waiting to be solved?"

---

# 4. Project Scope

The project focuses on discovering opportunities before solution design.

Included:

- Market observation
- Problem discovery
- Pattern recognition
- Opportunity identification
- Opportunity evaluation
- Prioritization
- Validation methodology

Excluded (for now):

- Product development
- Software architecture
- SaaS implementation
- AI implementation
- Marketing execution
- Sales processes

These topics may become future projects.

---

# 5. Research Philosophy

The methodology follows five principles.

## Principle 1

Problems before ideas.

---

## Principle 2

Evidence before assumptions.

---

## Principle 3

Patterns before isolated cases.

---

## Principle 4

Validation before construction.

---

## Principle 5

Methodology before technology.

# 8. Decision-Making Rules

Every important decision must satisfy three conditions.

1.

Supported by evidence.

2.

Logically consistent.

3.

Useful for future decision making.

---

# 10. Definition of Done

A phase is considered complete when:

- Its objective has been achieved.

- The key findings have been documented.

- Future improvements have been identified.

- The next phase has been clearly defined.

Perfect completeness is not required.

The project follows the Pareto Principle (80/20).

# 12. Long-Term Vision

The long-term objective is to establish OpportunityOS as an evidence-based discipline for discovering business opportunities.

The methodology should be applicable across industries, technologies, and countries.

The project aims to become technology-independent, continuously evolving while preserving its core principles.

---

# 13. Guiding Question

Every research activity should answer one question:

"Does this increase our ability to discover better business opportunities before building solutions?"

If the answer is no, the activity belongs outside the current scope.
